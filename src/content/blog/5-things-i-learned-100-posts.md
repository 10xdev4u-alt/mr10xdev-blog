5 things I learned from writing 100 blog posts. The
surprises, the lessons, the truth. The hard truths from
the trenches.

## Lesson 1: The first 10 are the hardest

The first 10 posts are the hardest. The first 10 posts
are the worst. The first 10 posts are the foundation.

**What I expected:** "I'll write great posts from day 1."

**What it was:** "The first 10 posts were bad. The 11th
was better. The 50th was good. The 100th was great."

The first 10 are the foundation. The foundation is the
practice. The practice is the progress.

**The lesson:** write the first 10. The first 10 are the
foundation.

## Lesson 2: The 80/20 is real

80% of the value comes from 20% of the posts. The 20% are
the popular posts. The 20% are the evergreen posts.

**What I expected:** "Every post will be popular. Every
post will be read."

**What it was:** "20% of my posts get 80% of the traffic.
The 20% are the ones I spent the most time on."

The 80/20 is real. The 80/20 is the truth. The 80/20 is
the leverage.

**The lesson:** focus on the 20%. The 20% are the
leverage.

## Lesson 3: The reader doesn't read carefully

The reader skims. The reader scans. The reader reads the
headings.

**What I expected:** "The reader will read every word. The
reader will think deeply."

**What it was:** "The reader skims. The reader reads the
headings. The reader reads the bold."

The reader is busy. The reader skims. The reader scans.

**The lesson:** design for the skim. The headings are the
truth.

## Lesson 4: The listicle is the format

The listicle is the most popular format. The listicle is
"5 ways to..." or "3 things...". The listicle is the truth.

**What I expected:** "Long-form essays are the most
popular."

**What it was:** "Listicles are the most popular. '5 ways
to...' gets 10x the traffic of a 2000-word essay."

The listicle is the format. The listicle is the truth.
The listicle is the leverage.

**The lesson:** write listicles. The listicle is the
leverage.

## Lesson 5: The consistency is the value

The value is not in any one post. The value is in the
100th post. The value is in the consistency.

**What I expected:** "The value is in the post. The value
is in the words."

**What it was:** "The value is in the 100th post. The
value is in showing up. The value is in the discipline."

The consistency is the value. The discipline is the value.
The showing up is the value.

**The lesson:** show up. The showing up is the value.

## The 5 lessons together

The 5 are the truth. The truth is the discipline. The
discipline is the writing.

| Lesson | What it taught me |
|---|---|
| First 10 are the hardest | Write the first 10 |
| 80/20 is real | Focus on the 20% |
| Reader skims | Design for the skim |
| Listicle is the format | Write listicles |
| Consistency is the value | Show up |

The lessons are the hard truths. The hard truths are the
lessons. The lessons are the discipline.

## The 80/20

80% of the value comes from:
- 80/20 is real (focus on the 20%)
- Consistency is the value (show up)

20% comes from:
- First 10 are the hardest (just start)
- Reader skims (design for the skim)
- Listicle is the format (use the format)

Focus on the 80% first. Add the 20% as you grow.

## The lesson

5 lessons. 1 career. 1 lesson: show up.

The writer that shows up is the writer that succeeds. The
writer that doesn't is the writer that fails.

The writing era is here. The showing up is the design.
The design is the discipline. The discipline is the
success.
